Issues We Solve with Walk-In Showers

Common Problems Fixed

We address the most common design and installation challenges Chandler homeowners face.

Inadequate waterproofing allowing water to escape into adjoining spaces - Professional waterproofing systems with proper membranes and barriers contain water completely.
Poor drainage causing standing water in shower - Linear drains or properly sloped floors ensure water flows away efficiently.
Inadequate ventilation leading to mold and moisture damage - Proper ventilation systems and exhaust fans remove excess moisture from open showers.
Safety concerns from slippery surfaces - Textured tile, grab bars, and proper lighting create a safe walk-in shower environment.

Keep It Looking New

Walk-In Shower Tips & Maintenance

Expert tips to maintain your walk-in shower and extend its lifespan in Chandler's climate.

Tip 1

Install a Linear Drain: Linear or trench drains handle water flow better than point drains and look sleek in modern showers.

Tip 2

Use Niche Storage: Built-in niches keep shampoo and soap organized without cluttering the floor.

Tip 3

Half-Glass Enclosures Work Great: Glass dividers contain some water splash while maintaining the open feel of walk-in designs.

Tip 4

Large Format Tile Looks Modern: 12x24 or 24x24 tiles create clean lines and fewer grout joints in walk-in showers.
